About

Ask about keyword

Get information about Twitter users' opinions on different topics. Enter a keyword of your choice. The app will search for tweets related to it, and it will select the most popular among them. Then, it will analyze the general opinion twords the topic and display the most frequently used adjectives.

Search for user

Enter a username in order to find out about its behaviour on Twitter. The information received consists of the most frequently adjectives used in its tweets, his overall attitude when posting, as well as the number of backlinks his profile has.

Technology we used

Front-end: HTML, CSS, JS, Bootstrap

Back-end: Python, Flask, NodeJS

APIs: Majestic's API, Google's Natural language processing API, Twitter's API
